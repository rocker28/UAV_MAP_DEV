UAV Mapping Technology

UAV Mapping refers to the use of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (drones) to capture aerial data, including images, videos, and LiDAR, which are processed to create detailed maps, 3D models, and geographic information system (GIS) data. This technology is widely used in industries like agriculture, construction, surveying, and environmental monitoring for accurate and efficient mapping and analysis.
